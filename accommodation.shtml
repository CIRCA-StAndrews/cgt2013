Accommodation and Food

Accommodation Information

Accommodation will be in McIntosh Hall.

Food

Breakfast and dinner will be served in McIntosh Hall. Breakfast will be served to participants on each morning from Monday to Friday. Dinner will be served on Sunday, Monday, Wednesday and Thursday evenings. The conference dinner will be on Tuesday.

Lunches will be provided at the Mathematical Institute, as will morning and afternoon tea/coffee with biscuits.
